The Whole Student

Real learning doesn't stop at the classroom door. Whether I'm on the sports field or in the boarding house, I'm building a community where students feel safe enough to take risks and grounded enough to own their choices.

High standards require high support.

My Approach

Belonging First

Every student should feel known and valued. I foster a culture where respect isn't just a rule - it's a habit - because how we treat our peers is as important as what we learn.

Clear Logic

Maths shouldn't be a mystery. I strip away the complexity to reveal the logic underneath, helping students solve the problem in front of them while building the thinking skills they'll use for life.

Whole Person & Growth

Growth happens everywhere - from the sports field to the boarding house. I challenge students to look past their current grade and envision the person they want to become.

Let's Connect

The best work happens when we do it together. If you want to swap teaching strategies, talk about mentorship, or just chat about how we help students thrive - my door is always open.

My Philosophy

Results start with connection. When a student feels seen, the effort follows. They might start by working hard because they trust you, but they finish by working hard because they finally believe in themselves.

My 4 Pillars of Practice

Radical Ownership

Character isn't taught; it's practiced. I encourage students to own their choices and to see their mistakes as the primary engine for growth.

Active Curiosity

By trading rote memorisation for 'aha!' moments, I turn high-pressure problems into puzzles the students can solve.

Relational Mentorship

Trust is built through shared experiences outside the classroom, allowing me to mentor the person, not just the student.

Purposeful Tech

Innovation should humanise education. I use digital tools to remove admin friction, leaving more room for connection.
